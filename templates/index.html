Have you ever been outside and needed to go really bad and wished you could just type something like “public bathrooms near me” into Google Maps?
PottyNYC is a web and mobile application that allows users to find public restrooms in NYC in real time.
This application tracks the location of the user and finds the nearest bathroom to you, and gives you directions to the bathroom.
